I enjoy exploring all facets of design! I'm still learning, and want to explore all that I can before I pick a favorite anything.

But for now, heres a few of my favorite typefaces, IN ORDER!

  1. Futura Condenced ExtraBold - anytime I use this typeface my friend tells me that it looks like a Nike ad. And maybe it does. But maybe that means there is a Nike position in my future
  2. Helvetica - The perfect all rounder. Simple? Maybe. But my biggest lesson this semester has been that less can be more.
  3. Anything Blackletter - Althought its a hard typeface group to make work due to its compexity, blackletter done right is heaven for my eyes. (thanks fontaines dc.)
